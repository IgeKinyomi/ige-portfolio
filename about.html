Get to Know Me!

Hi I'm Ige Kinyomi!

(ee-gay kin-yo-me)

I am a driven creative with a diverse style and various interests. Being an art major in the BA program and minoring in DMART (Digital Media Art) I have learned a plethora of skills. I can illustrate, animate, do graphic design, and much more. It is important that every artist learns and demonstrates various forms of media. I am an excellent communicator and seek to contribute my knowledge and skills in a studio (similar to Pixar or Laika) that offers growth and development opportunities. By having numerous skills, I am then able to perform any task needed. Look around to see my work!
